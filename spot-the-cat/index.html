Spot the Cat

Embark on an epic survival adventure where you battle against waves of relentless monsters in this thrilling action-packed game.

Game Overview

Spot the Cat is an engaging survival game that puts your strategic thinking and reflexes to the test. As you progress through increasingly challenging waves of monsters, you'll need to upgrade your character's abilities and choose the right weapons to survive.

Key Features

  • Intense wave-based combat system
  • Character progression and upgrades
  • Diverse monster types with unique abilities
  • Strategic gameplay elements
  • Engaging visual effects and animations

How to Play

Use your mouse or touch controls to move your character and attack monsters. Collect experience points to level up and choose powerful upgrades. Each wave brings new challenges and stronger enemies, so plan your strategy carefully to survive as long as possible.

Tips for Success

  • Focus on upgrading your core abilities first
  • Learn monster patterns and attack timing
  • Use the environment to your advantage
  • Manage your resources wisely
  • Experiment with different upgrade combinations
